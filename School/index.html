Amatshezulu High School is a public secondary school situated in the heart of Hammarsdale, an Urban Surburd within Pinetown, Kwazulu-Natal, South Africa.

Amatshezulu High school is a place of learning where student receive an ordinary education design to provide them with a well-rounded academic experience. The school is committed to fostering a comprehensive curriculum that equips students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in various aspect in life. This School is dedicated to provide quality education in the field of ordinary school curriculum.

The Schoool is a No-Fee-paying school dedicated to foster educational excellent in a supportive environment. Amatshezulu High School is classified within a quintile rating of 3, it endeavors to meet the educational needs of it's communinity while adhering to government guideline of basic education
